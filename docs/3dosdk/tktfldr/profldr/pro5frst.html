3DO PostPro Reference

This document is a reference for 3DO PostPro menus, windows, dialogs, and editors. The menus are discussed in the order they appear on the menu bar, from left to right. The windows, dialog boxes, and editors are covered in separate sections, but are discussed in relation to the menu options that bring them up.

Menu options that are for standard Macintosh functionality are not discussed. See your Macintosh user's manuals for this information.
