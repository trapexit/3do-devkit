Currently available CCMs

The 3DO Custom Code Modules (CCMs) for DeBabelizer let you do the following:

  • Convert a QuickTime movie or a folder of single-frame images into
  • 3DO cels
  • A 3DO animation (ANIM)
  • A 3DO streamed animation (SANM)

  • Convert a QuickTime movie or a folder of single-frame images into 8-bit coded cels.
The batch-processing capability of the CCM makes translating files from one format to another quick and easy.
